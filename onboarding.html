Welcome to TabCycle!

TabCycle helps you automatically switch between open tabs, perfect for monitoring dashboards, news feeds, or live updates on an external display.

Getting Started:
1. Open the tabs you want to cycle through.
2. Click the toolbar icon and select Start.
3. To stop, click the icon again and select Stop.

By default, tabs switch every 15 seconds (configurable), making it great for use on an external display, like a TV.

For the best experience, put Chrome in full-screen mode:
- Windows/Linux: Press F11
- Mac: Press Fn + F
